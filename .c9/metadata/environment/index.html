Comic Character Dashboard

\n
\n
\n

This dashboard features interactive charts to display data behind the story Comic Books Are Still Made By Men, For Men And About Men.\n All charts are interactive and can get the results filtered when you click on the chart except scatterplot.

\n

The gender Icons and the gender percentage are displayed on the top left side.The percentage will change accordingly when you click on the charts.

\n

The identity chart shows the number of characters who have different identities.

\n

The stacked bar chart displays the Alignment of the characters in relation to their gender.

\n

The scatterplot explains the number of appearance of each character in comic book and the year and the Alive barchart shows the number of alive and disesed characters.

\n

The table has the name and URL slug and the year the characters are first appeared in the books.

\n

All the original data is from Kaggle
